# Nine Years on Creekbluff

**An independent, neighborhood-driven investigation into a gutted, unoccupied house at 7609 Creekbluff Dr, Austin, TX 78750 — and a decade of Austin code-enforcement cases that never resolved it.**

🔗 **Read the report:** [wavecentral.github.io/7609-Creekbluff-Dr](https://wavecentral.github.io/7609-Creekbluff-Dr/)

---

## Purpose

This report documents the code-enforcement history of a single-family house on the Bull Creek greenbelt in Austin's District 10. The house has been gutted to the studs and unoccupied since roughly 2016, and has drawn City of Austin code complaints continuously since 2017.

It was produced by the president of the Bull Creek Home Owners Association — the lead correspondent with the City on this property since 2019 — to:

1. Assemble the verifiable record from public documents;
2. Separate confirmed facts from the neighborhood's contentions and from open questions;
3. Show how the property's ownership has moved among a small, connected set of people and, in 2025, into an LLC co-managed by its two prior owners of record; and
4. Give the City and District 10 office a single factual reference as an open Notice of Violation moves through its compliance window.

**This report is journalistic in nature. It is not advocacy material and it does not allege criminal conduct.** Where facts are established, they are cited to the underlying document. Where questions remain open, they are framed as questions.

## Key findings

### Confirmed (from public records)

- The interior was gutted to the studs, with exposed wiring and no active permits, when first inspected in **October 2017**; the owner repeatedly told inspectors that plans would be "submitted by Friday" — they were not.
- **Administrative Citation 002474** was posted in **July 2018** for work without a permit; the case then sat "awaiting an administrative hearing" through 2019.
- The chain of title runs **Gilyan & Johnson Investments LLC (2016) → Stephen W. & Angela D. Johnson (2018) → Michael Jeffrey Bueker (2022) → 7609 Creekbluff LLC (July 30, 2025)**.
- **7609 Creekbluff LLC** (Texas SOS filing **#806107975**, formed **July 2, 2025**) lists **Steve W. Johnson** and **Mike Bueker** as its two Managing Members, at the same address. The property was deeded into the LLC **28 days** after it was formed.
- An open **Notice of Violation CV‑2026‑067670** (June 2, 2026) is addressed to the LLC and carries a clause prohibiting sale/lease/transfer of the property, until compliance, without notifying the City and any transferee.

### Open questions for the City

- Was a contested administrative hearing on the 2017–2018 violations ever held?
- On what basis was the 2023 code case closed as "voluntary compliance," when public permit records showed no permits on file?
- Has the structure ever been formally evaluated against the unsafe-/vacant-structure and attractive-nuisance provisions the HOA cited, rather than only exterior-maintenance items?

## A note on privacy

Years of correspondence among neighbors, HOA board members, and City officials underlie this report. To keep private individuals' personal contact information out of the public record, that correspondence is **summarized and quoted in the report, not reproduced as raw files.** Only public City records and public filings are published as exhibits. The raw source material is deliberately excluded from this repository (see `.gitignore`).
